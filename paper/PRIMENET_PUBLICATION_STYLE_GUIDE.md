# PrimeNet Publication Style Guide v2.3

v2.3 focuses on manuscript integration. The generated DOCX should read like a paper, not merely an appendix of assets.

## Rules

- Figures and tables appear where they support the section narrative.
- Canonical terms are used consistently: Prime Space, Repository, Observatory, Observation Session, Product, Atlas, Registry, Publisher.
- Implementation numbers are architectural evidence, not mathematical results.
- The paper remains calm, technical, and engineering-oriented.
